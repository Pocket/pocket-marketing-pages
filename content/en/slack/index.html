Save to Pocket from Slack

With Pocket for Slack, you’ll have a quiet, dedicated place to save links to articles, videos, and other interesting stories your teammates share throughout the day. When you’re ready, the things you’ve saved will be there for you in Pocket on your phone, tablet, or computer.

Get started

1

When you want to save a link that a teammate has shared, click the message actions button and select Save to Pocket.

2

If it’s your first time saving, Pocket will send you a message offering to Connect Pocket to Slack.

3

Sign in to Pocket, and connect your account. Now you’ll be able to save any link that’s been posted in Slack.
